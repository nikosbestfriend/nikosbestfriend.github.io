About Me

Outside of research, I am an active volunteer firefighter with the Lorton Volunteer Fire Department in Virginia, where I also serve on the Board of Directors. I find deep meaning in serving my community and being part of a team that shows up for others.

When I am not working or volunteering, you can find me skateboarding or out on the water fishing. These hobbies keep me grounded and remind me that the best discoveries happen when you slow down and pay attention.
